Discussion guide template

Try to create a mix of closed questions and open questions. An example of a closed question could be, 'who was your favourite character?' and an example of a closed question could be 'how did you feel after watching/reading the material?' or 'what did you think of the decision to include X or Y in the material?'.

All questions are optional - they are inteded as starting points for discussions.

Lastly - when creating these, be careful to avoid writing questions that contain spoilers!
