Discussion guide for 'Disability Visibility'

Below are some suggestions of questions to help facilitate a 30 minute discussion on the book, 'Disability Visibility'.

All questions are optional - they are inteded as starting points for discussions.

Lastly - when creating these, be careful to avoid writing questions that contain spoilers!

  • What was your favourite essay?
  • Which essay did you find the most challenging?
  • Which essayist would you most like to go for coffee and a chat with?
  • What things did you learn, that you'd not previously known?
