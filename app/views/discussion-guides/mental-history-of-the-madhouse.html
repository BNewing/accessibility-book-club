Discussion guide for 'Mental: Inside the Madhouse'

Below are some suggestions of questions to help facilitate a 30 minute discussion on the documentary, 'Mental: a history of the madhouse'.

  • How did watching the documentary make you feel? What were your initial thoughts?
  • Who's story shocked you the most in the documentary?
  • What community support is currently available for mental health issues? What treatment is currently on offer for mental illness in the UK?
  • The document talks about abuse of power vs treatment - lithium, "insulin treatment", ECT and "thump therapy". What do you think allowed this to happen? Ego - carers vs curers? How do we know current practises are ethical?
  • What do you think to R.D.Lang's view that madness is a normal reaction to the mad world we live in?
