Discussion guide for 'Mission: Accessible'

Below are some suggestions of questions to help facilitate a 30 minute discussion on the film, 'Mission: Accessible'.

  • Did you like this film?
  • What feelings did the film evoke for you?
  • Did anything particular in the film shock you, or stand out as something you'd not known before?'
  • Which person in the film would you most like to meet?
  • If you could hear this same story from another person's point of view, who would you choose?
