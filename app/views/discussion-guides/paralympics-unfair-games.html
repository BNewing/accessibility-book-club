Discussion guide for 'Paralympics: the unfair games?'

Below are some suggestions of questions to help facilitate a 30 minute discussion on the documentary, 'Paralympics: the unfair games?'.

  • How would you design a classification system?
  • What did you think of the 'superhuman' phrasing that was discussed in the documentary?
  • Why do you think the UK Athletics and IPO organisations were so closed off?
