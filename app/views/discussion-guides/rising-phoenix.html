Discussion guide for 'Rising Phoenix'

Below are some suggestions of questions to help facilitate a 30 minute discussion on the film, 'Rising Phoenix'.

  • Have you been to the Paralympic games before, or watched it? What is your favourite event?
  • What did you learn from the film that you'd not known before?
  • Which athletic would you most like to go for coffee with? Or train with?
  • The documentary talked about bringing including disabled people in high school sports into federal law in the US - what are your experiences of that in the UK?
