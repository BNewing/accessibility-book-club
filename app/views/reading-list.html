Reading list

This is a collection of books, films, documentaries and podcasts on the topic of accessibility.

This list aims to focus on books that centre disabled people, or disability, rather than accessibiity as a technical topic. It also aims to focus on books created by disabled writers.

Inclusion of the media on the list doesn't necessarily endorse the views presented it on it. It just suggests it as a starting point for a discussion.
