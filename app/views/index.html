Suggestion for how to structure your book club

A structure that has tested well is 30 minute sessions, once a month, on an online platform like Hangouts or Meet. Attendees suggesting books/podcasts/films, and then us voting to decide which one to discuss also works well.

It works well to have a designated facilitator per session, too, who is responsible for calendar admin, creating or finding discussion guides, and creating the poll to decide what we discuss next week.
